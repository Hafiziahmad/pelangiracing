Welcome to PELANGI RACING

EXTREME FUNRIDE MOTORCROSS

PELANGI RACING

Looking for a new challenge that will truly test your dirt bike skills? Let’s visit us Pelangi Racing in Kluang, Johor Malaysia, tailor a ride that you will never forget

  • Incredibly diverse terrain will ignite your inner hard enduro passion.
  • Explore the the nature jungle with a real expert, and go home with a story to share with all your buddies.
  • Give us a call today to discuss the possibilities for your guided dirt bike tour.

We’re passionate about motocross and dedicated to providing an exciting experience for riders of all levels. Whether you’re a beginner or a pro, our tracks and friendly team are here to help you enjoy the thrill of the ride.

We offer top-quality equipment, safety gear, and a supportive community where you can connect with fellow riders. Join us for fun events, guided tours, and unforgettable memories. Let’s hit the tracks together and embrace the adrenaline of motocross!
